Peru

Peru is a South American country with a rich and diverse culture, history, and geography. Peru has the legacy of the Incas, the biodiversity of the Amazon, the Andes, and the coast, and the democracy of a republic. Peru has a population of over 35 million people, who speak Spanish and other languages. Peru has a dynamic and diverse economy, based on mining, agriculture, tourism, and manufacturing.
