Splash: White Water Rafting

Our mission is to provide you with an unforgettable experience of whitewater rafting on some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the world. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, we have a trip that suits your level of adventure and skill.

History

Splash: White Water Rafting is a company that was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate rafters who wanted to share their love for the sport with others. They started with offering trips on the Colorado River in the Grand Canyon, one of the most spectacular and challenging whitewater destinations in the world. They quickly gained popularity among adventure seekers and nature lovers, who appreciated their professionalism, enthusiasm, and respect for the environment.

Since then, Splash: White Water Rafting has expanded its operations to include other rivers in the United States and abroad, such as the Arkansas River in Colorado, the Zambezi River in Africa, and the Futaleuf√∫ River in Chile. They have also developed a reputation for providing high-quality equipment, safety standards, and customer service. Their mission is to create unforgettable experiences for their clients, while promoting conservation and education about the rivers they explore.
