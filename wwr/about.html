Splash: White Water Rafting

Our mission is to provide you with an unforgettable experience of whitewater rafting on some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the world. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, we have a trip that suits your level of adventure and skill.

History

Splash: White Water Rafting is a company that was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate rafters who wanted to share their love for the sport with others. They started with offering trips on the Colorado River in the Grand Canyon, one of the most iconic and spectacular whitewater destinations in the world. Since then, they have also developed a reputation for providing high-quality equipment, safety standards, and customer service.
